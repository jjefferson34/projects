Handy Substitutions

Butter: half peanut butter and half oil
Vanilla extract: same amount of maple syrup
Buttermilk: to a cup of milk add 1 tbsp of lemon juice or vinegar, let stand for 5 mins
Heavy cream: ¾ milk, ¼ butter
Brown sugar: 1 tbsp maple syrup to 1c white sugar
Mascarpone: 4 cream cheese to 1 heavy cream
Cream of tartar: same amount of lemon juice or white vinegar
high fructose corn syrup: same amount of apple cider
