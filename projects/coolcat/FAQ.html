FAQ

What is Coolcat for?

 Coolcat is for providing an easy way to access many recipes that are used for cooking.
Why should I use Coolcat when I can just search up the recipes I want online? We at Coolcat eliminate unnecessary results to enure you only get recipes, and nothing else.

How do I use Coolcat?

 You just simply type in the recipe youre interested in into the searchbar, and form there you can look at recipes from multiple sites, and save the recipes you want to use.
Do you have recipes that are for vegitarians, vegans, and other people with specific dietary needs?
Coolcat has links to websites that have recipes for indivisuals with dietary specific needs. You can also put your dietary need in the search bar before your recipe search.

Where can I go if i have an issue making a recipe provided by Coolcat?
If you are having trouble with a recipe, you can go to the Coolcat forum that is linked in the tips page and post your question there for someone to answer. The forum can also be use to chat about food and to post your creations.
